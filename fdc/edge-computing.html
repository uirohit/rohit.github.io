Edge Computing Services

We offer cutting-edge Edge Computing Solutions

At Fortune Data Center, we offer cutting-edge Edge Computing Solutions designed to bring processing power closer to where data is generated, enabling faster and more efficient data handling.


Services We Offered

Our services are tailored to meet the needs of various industries, providing enhanced performance, reduced latency, and greater reliability. Here’s a closer look at the Edge Computing Solutions we provide:


Content Delivery Network

Enhance your digital content delivery with our robust CDN services. We provide a network of distributed servers that deliver content to users based on their geographic location.

Ensuring faster load times and improved user experience. Our CDN solutions are ideal for websites, streaming services, and online platforms seeking to optimise content delivery.

Over-the-Top (OTT) Services

Transform your media delivery with our OTT services. We offer comprehensive solutions for streaming video, audio, and other media content directly to consumers over the internet.

Our OTT services provide high-quality, scalable, and secure streaming capabilities, enabling you to reach a broader audience with your digital content.




Gaming & AR/VR

Deliver immersive gaming and augmented/virtual reality experiences with our edge computing solutions.



We provide the low-latency and high-performance infrastructure required to support real-time gaming and AR/VR applications. Our solutions enhance user experiences by reducing lag and ensuring smooth, responsive gameplay and interactions.

AI Services

Leverage the power of artificial intelligence with our AI services. We provide advanced AI solutions that enable you to process and analyse data at the edge, delivering real-time insights and automation.

Our AI services support various applications, including predictive maintenance, customer behaviour analysis, and intelligent automation, enhancing your business operations.

IoT & Edge Computing

Optimise your Internet of Things (IoT) deployments with our managed IoT and edge computing services. We offer end-to-end solutions for managing IoT devices and processing data at the edge, reducing latency and improving efficiency.

Our services ensure seamless connectivity, robust security, and efficient management of your IoT infrastructure.


Analytics

Gain valuable insights from your data with our edge analytics services. We provide advanced analytics capabilities that enable you to process and analyse data at the edge, delivering real-time insights and actionable information.

Our analytics solutions support various use cases, including predictive analytics, real-time monitoring, and decision-making support.
